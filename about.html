A little bit about myself 🌸

Life's journey led me to Germany and I embraced this new chapter with open arms. I am Lara, a 23-year-old with a heart full of dreams and a passion for front-end engineering.

Munich became my second index, not by chance, but by choice. It's a place that resonated with my quest for better opportunities and a desire to experience a different corner of the world. Rather than pursuing costly studies in other English-speaking countries, I decided to learn the german language and create my own path. I passed the German language test in São Paulo and got a Visa in Rio de Janeiro, what opened the doors to this vibrant and international city.

My background in Communications & Design, coupled with a fascination for psychology, laid the foundation for my transformation into the world of Web Development. From the very beginning, I discovered affinity for understanding user needs, designing interfaces and bringing it to life on screens.

I'm an eternal learner, always expanding my knowledge and skill set. While I may not have mastered programming, I'm excited to begin a journey with hands-on experience where I can grow not only as a front-end engineer but also as an individual. I'm ready to explore the world of web development, where I can design a better version of digital products.
